Cindy Liao Rasamoelina, 1993.
Born in Madagascar, Paris based.


Liao Rasamoelina slowly hand-builds stoneware objects.

Using mostly the pinching technique, the shapes acquire organic outlines, making each ceramic truly unique.


She considers her pieces as embodiments of a series of actions.

Sometimes repeating the same order of steps without reaching or aiming for the initial result.


Taking a deep dive into chemistry, she started developing her own glazes.
