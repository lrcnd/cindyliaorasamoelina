Cindy Liao Rasamoelina, 1993.
Born in Madagascar, Paris based.


Liao Rasamoelina hand-builds utilitarian stoneware objects.

She considers her pieces as embodiments of a series of actions.

Sometimes repeating the same order of steps without reaching or aiming for the initial result.


Her shapes stem from her dual culture, being Malagasy of Chinese descent.

She deliberately integrates contingency as part of her practice.

The clay she works with and her glazing process intentionally reduces her control over her work's final look.
